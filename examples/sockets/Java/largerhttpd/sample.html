Biography

  • 2000--present. Associate Professor and Graduate Program Coordinator of Computer Science, College of Engineering, Boise State University.
  • May 2008--Dec 2008. Consulting Scientist. Balihoo, Inc.
  • Jan. 2007-- May. 2008. Part-time Chief Scientist. Balihoo, Inc.
  • July 2007--Dec 2008. Interim Chair of Computer Science Department, College of Engineering, Boise State University.
  • 1994--2000. Assistant Professor of Computer Science, Boise State University.
  • Jan. 1992--Jul. 1994. Research Assistant. Center for Parallel Computation, in the School of Computer Science at University of Central Florida. Evaluation, installation and maintenance of various parallel systems.
  • August 1987--August 1988, Dec. 1988, Summer 1989. Worked at converting High Energy Physics Applications to run on parallel clusters on the Advanced Computing project at Fermi National Accelerator Laboratory.
    •

Research

Research Interests: Parallel systems and languages, Beowulf clusters, parallel system hardware/software design, parallel application development,  parallel algorithms and data structures.

Director of the Beowulf Cluster Lab (funded by National Science Foundation Award No. 0321233)

Organizer of the First IEEE Idaho High Performance Computing Workshop.
